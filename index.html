Services

R.C.T

(R.C.T)Root canal treatment is needed when an injury or a large cavity damages the tooth's root. The root becomes infected or inflamed.

Cosmetic Filling

Any dental work that improves the appearance of teeth,gum or bite

Ultrasonic Scaling

It removes dental plaque/ calculus rapidly from teeth.

Crown and Bridge work

Both crowns and most bridges are fixed prosthetic devices. Unlike removable devices such as dentures, which you can take out and clean daily.

Complete and partial denture work

Replacement of teeth attached to a pink plastic base or metal frame work

Surgical Extraction

If a more volatile tooth has yet to grow in, however, your dentist needs to remove gum tissue or bone in order to extract it. This is called a surgical extraction

Children Dentistry

Dealing with children's teeth from birth to adolescence.

Teeth Whitening

Cosmetic procedure that helps to whiten discolored teeth

Orthodontics

Orthodontics is the branch of dentistry that corrects teeth and jaws that are positioned improperly. Crooked teeth and teeth that do not fit together correctly are harder to keep clean.

Gum Treatment

To eliminate the infection from gum by various treatment modalities
